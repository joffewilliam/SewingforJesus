Making dresses for girls — sharing love through sewing

Started by Bev Breece, Sewing for Jesus meets on Mondays from 10:30 AM – 2:30 PM at Evangel Baptist Church in Taylor, Michigan. We sew dresses & skirts for needy children around the world as well as here in the States, pack them into Operation Christmas Child shoe boxes, and ship them to many countries.

We also welcome trades of items so everyone can pack shoe boxes and bless children everywhere. All of us share the same purpose and plan God has led us to: to show God's love in a very special way.

Meeting location: Evangel Baptist Church — 16994 Telegraph Rd, Taylor, MI 48180

Our Mission

At Sewing for Jesus, we believe in the power of hands-on service. Through sewing and crafting, we provide clothing to children in need, fostering community and spreading hope. Join us in making a difference, one stitch at a time.

"For I was hungry and you gave me something to eat, I was thirsty and you gave me something to drink, I was a stranger and you invited me in, I needed clothes and you clothed me..." — Matthew 25:35-36
